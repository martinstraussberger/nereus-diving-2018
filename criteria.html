NEREUS Criteria System

We have assessed each dive resort and dive center based on multiple criteria to ensure NEREUS offers you the best and most unique diving experience.


Being scuba-divers ourselves, we understand the importance of transparency and trustworthiness when it comes to booking dives. All listings on NEREUS had to pass the following criteria:

1. Trusted reviews from multiple providers

We are screening the assessments from dedicated review partners such as Tripadvisor, Holidaycheck, Google Review.

2. Highest quality

All partners have to provide excellent quality in terms of accommodation, dive sites, equipment, and instructors.

3. Awards for extraordinary achievements.

We are looking into achievements, and awards our partners have accomplished throughout the recent years.

4. Sustainability efforts.

NEREUS only cooperates with dive resorts and dive centers that value sustaining the ocean ecosystem and moreover contribute with sustainable initiatives.

5. Offer Unique and exclusive experience.

Obviously this is hard to measure, but it comes down to selecting only partners that can offer that little bit of extra.

6. Reasonable Group Sizes

Our partners guarantee that they do not overbook their diving groups.
