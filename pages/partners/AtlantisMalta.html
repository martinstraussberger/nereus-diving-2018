Gozo is known as one of the best diving destinations in the Mediterranean. The mild climate of the island makes it possible to dive the beautiful reefs, caves, cliffs, wrecks, and the famous 'Blue Hole' all year round. The Atlantis is a family run company and has been bringing divers from all over the world to Malta's most beautiful diving spots since 1993. The team consists of well-qualified instructors who offer you all kinds of diving education, from beginner courses to technical diving with mixed gases.
