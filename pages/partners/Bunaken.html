Bunaken Oasis Dive Resort and Spa

Learn more about Bunaken Resort:

Bunaken Oasis redefines diving and relaxation. You can expect a 5-star adventure resort, which at the same time attaches great importance to the ecological footprint. The resort offers 12 large, traditionally built villas, including a two-bedroom family villa and a villa near the Long House for those who may have difficulty walking. A highlight of the resort is an infinity pool with deck chairs and easy access to the bar for those who want to spend time above and not under the water. In addition to a chill-out cocktail bar and a full-service restaurant serving Indonesian and international cuisine, amenities include an air-conditioned library, a fully-equipped classroom in the dive center with 4k flat-screen TV, and a spacious camera room with more loading points.
