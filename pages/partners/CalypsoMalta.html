Learn more about: Hotel Calypso

The Calypso is an impressive hotel with 100 rooms near Marsalforn Bay. Located directly on the sea, the hotel offers a great view of the Mediterranean Sea. Among other amenities, this hotel offers a private swimming pool and wellness programs. The best diving school in Malta, Atlantis Diving Center, is just 900m from the hotel and can be reached by foot in 12 minutes.
